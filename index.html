Centripetal Tic-Tac-Toe

8×8 board · get 4 in a row (rows, columns, or diagonals) · vs. computer
X to move
Placement rule: a piece must touch the edge of the board, or connect to an edge by an unbroken straight orthogonal line of pieces — a gap-free row or column run from the border in to your cell (no diagonals). The board fills inward from the rim toward the center.
